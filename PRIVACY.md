# Privacy Policy

AIChatAssistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or conversation content.

## Permissions used

- **Host permissions** (AI platforms): Used solely to read conversation content displayed in your browser for generating a local table of contents. No data is sent to our servers.
- **Host permissions** (API endpoints): Used to forward your API key and conversation snippets to the AI provider you have configured (e.g. OpenAI, DeepSeek). This communication is directly between your browser and the API provider.
- **Storage**: Used to save your API key and preferences locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-party APIs

If you choose to enable AI summaries, short excerpts of conversation text are sent to the API provider you select. Please refer to that provider's privacy policy for how they handle your data.

## Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on this repository.
